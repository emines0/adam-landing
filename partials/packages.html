FAQ: Do I need a full gym?

No. The plan is written around your equipment (home, gym, or hybrid). If you have dumbbells and a bench, that's enough to progress.

FAQ: Do you do meal plans?

We use targets + templates (and optional examples). The focus is sustainability, flexibility, and learning what works for you.

FAQ: How fast will I see results?

Most clients notice energy, consistency, and strength improvements in 2–3 weeks. Visible changes typically follow over 6–12+ weeks depending on goal and adherence.
