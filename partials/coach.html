About

I'm a previous professional football player, that has been working as a personal trainer since 2014 and have become the most selling PT in one of the biggest gym chain in Norway and Slovakia.

Throughout my career, I became the top-selling personal trainer within one of the largest gym chains operating in Norway and Slovakia. My coaching experience ranges from elite athletes across various sports to individuals looking to transform their lifestyle, improve fitness, and maintain long-term health. I have great experience with professional athletes within various sports, but also regarding regular clients with need to change or keep up a healthy lifestyle.

Experiences

Football: previous professional football player, football coach and physical trainer. Personal trainer for football players in Premier League, Bundesliga, Eliteserien, Major Soccer League, Ekstraklasa, Slovak & Czech first league.

Other sports: personal trainer for professional MMA fighters, tennis players, handball players, and basketball players. I have wide experience when it comes to rehabilitation, technique training, imbalances, weight loss, lifestyle changes, and hypertrophy training.
